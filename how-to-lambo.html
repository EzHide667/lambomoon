1. MetaMask

1st step is to create an account on the official Metamask website
It supports Chrome & Firefox : https://metamask.io/download.html

Always double check the address when someone link you to this website : it's subject to lot of scams since it's a money related company and software, which attracs a lot of people including hackers and scammers unfortunately.

Click on the blue "download" button

This will redirect you to the Google Play store
Click "Add to Chrome"

It's now installed !
Follow ahead with the "Get Started" button

Time to create a wallet !

Here is also the interface to recover your wallet from the seed that will be given to you in next steps !

Here click "Decline" to not send analytic data to MetaMask
It's a personal choice against KYC but you're free on that - and anything else ofc.

Then create a password
This one is related on your local device to use your wallet.

Then there's a video about how to secure your wallet

Now the famous Seed Phrase
