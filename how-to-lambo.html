MetaMask & BSC

1st step is to create an account on the official Metamask website
It supports Chrome & Firefox : https://metamask.io/download.html

Always double check the address when someone link you to this website : it's subject to lot of scams since it's a money related company and software, which attracs a lot of people including hackers and scammers unfortunately.

Click on download : You will be asked to save a randomly generated phrase containing 8 to 12 words !

Save these the most securely possible (on offline harddrive for eg.)
These are the keys of your wallet !
We consider any user desktop setup compromised

The highest level of security acheivable within the Cryptocurrency wallet landscape are "paper wallets". Then saving the secret seed (private key) written on metal.
Doesn't hurt aswell to have few copies on paper.

Consider computers can fail, and Network aswell.

Not your keys, not your coins
